Airplane Ticket Price Perdiction Tool

Required Inputs:

  • Quarter: 1, 2, 3, or 4
  • Origin: origin airport abbreivation such as BOS for Boston Logan International Airprt
  • Dest: destination airport abbreivation such as LAX for Los Angeles International Airportt
  • Number of Tickets: number of tickets per order
  • Airline Company: airline company abbreivation such as AA for American Airline

Sample Input: Quarter,Origin,Dest,NumberOfTickets,AirlineCompany 1,BOS,LAX,2,AA

Product Page:

  1. Scroll down to GET /PricePredict/{plan} and expand the tab.
  2. Click on the Try it out button at the upper right hand corner.
  3. In the Parameters section, click on the text box, and enter your trip information. Sample input: 1,BOS,LAX,2,AA
  4. Scroll down to the Code Section, and see the Response Body, you will see your Price Per Ticket prediction.
